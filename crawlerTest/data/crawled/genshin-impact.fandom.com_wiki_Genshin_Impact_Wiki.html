Welcome to the Genshin Impact Wiki!

About Official Website HoYoLAB Community Forums Official News Manga

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG developed and published by HoYoverse for the PC, iOS/Android, PS4/PS5, and Xbox Series X|S platforms.
The game is free-to-play with a gacha monetization system in the form of Wishes.
This wiki is an English resource for information about the global version of the game. There are unmarked spoilers on this wiki.
This wiki currently has 34,183 articles and 278,660 files.

Please read the Community Page and Rules before editing and discussing!
